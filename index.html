Demographic Info

Select the Subject ID from the dropdown above, and this panel displays the Subject's Demographic Info.

The Bar Chart displays the bacterial cultures that appear within the Subject's sample. Only the top ten (most populous) cultures are displayed.

Hover your mouse pointer over any of the chart bars to see which bacterial cultures account for the sample value.

The Gauge displays the Subject's belly button washing frequency. This information tends to correlate with the number of cultures that reside inside the belly button.

In general, less washing yields a greater range of cultures.

The Bubble Chart displays the total bacterial population that was detected within the Subject's sample. The bubble size is proportional to the population of a given bacterial strain.

    Chart Controls

  • Hover your mouse pointer over any of the chart bubbles to see which bacterial cultures account for the sample value.
  • Use your mouse wheel to zoom in or out, or click and drag to zoom into an area of interest.
  • Use the Reset axes control on the Mode bar to reset the chart display.
